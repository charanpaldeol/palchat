Privacy on the Internet Must Be a Right of Every Human

Big corporations store and analyze our data, then target us with ads or content that benefits their profits instead of our well-being. This imbalance in power and transparency needs to be addressed by policy, awareness, and product design.

Credit Card and Debt Cycle

We are encouraged to use credit cards for convenience. But over time, that ease turns into habit—and soon, a cycle of overspending and debt begins. Even when we pay our bills, the loop continues. There's no doubt technology has improved our lives, but society hasn't done enough to teach people how to use these tools responsibly.
